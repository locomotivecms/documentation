---
title: How do I change my password?
order: 2
---

Once you’re logged in, click your avatar or name at the top right corner of your screen and
click Account Settings.

![219](pages/how-do-i-change-my-password/D4wD5jm2SnuM4ljCwZHk_account-locomotivecms.png)

Click Credentials.

![489](pages/how-do-i-change-my-password/YPq4CseGSc23qGom8QhU_Credentials-locomotivecms.png)

Change your password.

![481](pages/how-do-i-change-my-password/4WMyZPqMQwymrCfi8x74_change-password.png)

Don’t forget to save your changes
