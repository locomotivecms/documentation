title: How do I change the language of my user interface ?
order: 1
---

## 1. First Step

Once you’re logged in, click your avatar or name at the top right corner of your screen and
click Account Settings.

![219](pages/how-do-i-change-the-language-of-my-user-interface/liOhvAaRTzK4tDYSG1VQ_account-locomotivecms.png)

## 2. Second Step

Click UI language and select your language in the drop down menu.

![495](pages/how-do-i-change-the-language-of-my-user-interface/BubYGcwvRtuZ0QHji4ON_change-20UI-20language.png)

Available UI languages are English, Dutch, Greek, French and Brazilian Portuguese.

Click Save.

Please note that if your language isn’t available yet, [you can help us translate it from English to your native language](https://www.transifex.com/locomotive/locomotive-engine/).
